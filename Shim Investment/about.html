About Us

SHIM Investment (Pty) Ltd is a proudly South African construction and engineering company established in 2022 and based in Ndwedwe, KwaZulu-Natal. The company was founded with a clear vision of delivering reliable infrastructure solutions while contributing meaningfully to community development and economic growth.

We operate as a CIDB Grade 1 CE and 1 GB contractor, specializing in civil engineering and general building projects. Our services are executed in strict compliance with industry regulations, safety standards, and quality requirements to ensure consistent and dependable results for our clients.

SHIM Investment is a 100% black-owned enterprise led by its founder and director, Mr. Khumulani Mxolisi Maphumulo. Coming from a previously disadvantaged background and living with a disability, the director’s personal journey has shaped the company’s strong commitment to inclusivity, empowerment, and equal opportunity within the construction industry.

Our approach is grounded in integrity, accountability, and professionalism. We strive to build long-term partnerships by delivering quality workmanship, adhering to timelines, and maintaining transparent communication throughout every project.

Our Mission

Our mission at SHIM Investment is to:

•Provide reliable and innovative construction and engineering solutions.

•Foster community development by creating job opportunities and building sustainable infrastructure.

•Uphold a commitment to inclusivity, empowerment, and excellence in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading and trusted partner in the construction and engineering industry, recognized for delivering exceptional services while uplifting communities and embracing diversity.
