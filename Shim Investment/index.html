Welcome to Shim Investment

Shim Investment (Pty) Ltd is a proudly South African construction and engineering company established in 2022 and based in Ndwedwe, KwaZulu-Natal. We are a CIDB Grade 1 CE and 1 GB contractor, offering reliable and professional services in civil engineering, general building, and project management.

Our company is founded on the principles of integrity, inclusivity, and community empowerment. As a 100% black-owned enterprise led by a director living with a disability, we are deeply committed to creating opportunities for sustainable development while delivering infrastructure solutions that meet industry standards and client expectations.

At SHIM Investment, we take pride in our workmanship, compliance, and attention to detail. Whether executing small-scale projects or contributing to larger developments, our goal is to build lasting partnerships and deliver value-driven results that support economic growth and community upliftment.

Why Choose Us

Choosing SHIM INVESTMENT (PTY) LTD means partnering with a company that values:
